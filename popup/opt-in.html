Ad Limiter Opt-In

Ad Limiter just upgraded to a new version. To comply with new disclosure policies, Ad Limiter must ask for your permission to continue requesting rating information from the Sitetruth rating service. Ad Limiter is not changing what it does.

Ad Limiter rates web sites. That rating is used to pick the one best ad to show. Ad Limiter also rates the sites in search results and shows rating icons.

So Ad Limiter needs your permission to send domain names seen in search results to sitetruth.com to get the site's rating. Your information is used for no other purpose than that lookup.
